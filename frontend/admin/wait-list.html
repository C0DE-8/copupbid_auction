Cancel & Refund Waitlist

Cancels queued / in_progress waitlist rows for a product & mode and refunds bid_points to users.

This will:
  • Lock all eligible waitlist rows for that product & mode.
  • Refund bid_points per user.
  • Mark those rows as cancelled.
